Exploring the philosophy of machine reasoning—how large language models seem to understand, remember, and evolve through context.

What to expect

I write about the emerging philosophy of large language models—where language, context, and computation meet. This is philosophical investigation grounded in real model behavior, asking how machines simulate reasoning and what that reveals about us.

  • What does it mean for an AI to “understand”?
  • Can probability become insight?
  • Where does reasoning emerge in machines built to predict?
