Project

Whats My Art Worth

whatsmyartworth.com • SEO lead gen engine

Whats My Art Worth is an SEO authority site built entirely with AI to capture art valuation intent and convert it into acquisition opportunities. It educates owners on valuation from photos and funnels high-intent visitors into a free online valuation flow, turning organic search into qualified art leads.

Specs

  • Purpose: SEO-first lead generation for art valuation and acquisition.
  • Content Engine: AI-authored pillar + cluster pages with structured data.
  • Primary CTA: Photo-based valuation upload flow.

Stack + Tools

  • Indexing + Ranking: Google Search Console | GA4
  • OpenAI Tools: API | Codex | Atlas
  • AI Tools: Adobe Firefly
  • Hosting: Digital Ocean App | Cloudflare

Analytics snapshot

In just six weeks since launch, and prior to full indexing, the site is already ranking for 22 keywords, five of which are on Google’s first page. A performance update will be provided in six months (today is 01/27/2026).
